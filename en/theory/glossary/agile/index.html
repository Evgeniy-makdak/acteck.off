Agile methodology

MethodologyAgileprovides flexible management of software development projects. Particularly effective when working in small teams.

The Agile methodology system also includesSCRUMAndKanban.

The Agile methodology is based on the following principles:

  • Clients must be satisfied with the timeliness and quality of the developed software and its updates.
  • The development must be flexible and make all necessary changes in the shortest possible time.
  • It is necessary to ensure frequent software updates for clients, each update should be released at least once every2–16weeks
  • Collaboration in a team of managers and developers throughout the entire software work cycle.
  • Encouraging motivated developers.
  • Priority of personal conversation over other methods of transmitting information.
  • Evaluation of the effectiveness of the work process is ensured only by the sale of a quality product, and not by hours, labor costs, etc.
  • The development of any team is based on the active use of flexible processes.
  • For a developed product, not only technical aspects, but also design should be priorities.
  • The desire to reduce the amount of unnecessary work and simplify work processes.
  • The priority when organizing the work process should be self-organization and initiative.
  • The team constantly evaluates the effectiveness of its work and corrects possible shortcomings.
