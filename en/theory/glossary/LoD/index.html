LoD ruleset

Set of rulesLoDfor programming (Law of Demeter, Law of Demeter) suggests that software development should limit interactions between different system components.

According to LoD rules, every component in your system must:

  • Have limited knowledge about the operation and functionality of other modules and components.
  • Interact only with those authorized components and be isolated from working with unfamiliar modules.
