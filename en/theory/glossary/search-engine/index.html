What is PS (search engine)

AbbreviationPSdenotesearch engines- programs and sites that are designed for the user to search for the information he needs on the Internet.

At the moment, there are a large number of search engines, incl. and narrowly targeted ones, helping to find, for example, certain photos or music. However, two PS remain the most popular in the Russian-speaking segment - Yandex and Google, each of which has its own algorithms and features that allow it to take into account the diverse preferences of users.
