The principle of shared responsibility

Principlesharing of responsibilitiesimplies the allocation of functional blocks in the developed project that solve specialized problems.

The great advantage of applying the principle of separation of responsibilities is that the complexity of the workflow is reduced while at the same time its reliability and flexibility (adaptability) are increased.

The simplest example of using the principle of separation of concerns is the separation of functions between classes and objects in OOP.
