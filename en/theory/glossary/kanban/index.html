Kanban methodology

MethodologyKanban(translated from Japanese as “billboard, sign”) facilitates the implementation of the product being developed within the time period specified by the customer, while the load is evenly distributed among all project participants.

The use of Kanban ensures transparency of the workflow, since all incoming and completed tasks are clearly displayed on a special board - physical (board with sticky notes) or virtual (Trello).

The Kanban methodology includes the following principles:

  • The workflow is based on existing development methods that can be improved and modified according to the tasks at hand.
  • All important changes in the project occur according to a pre-planned agreement between team members.
  • All team members must respect the existing order, roles and responsibilities of each.
  • The initiative of all team members should be encouraged.
