PascalCase naming style

Writing style of compound namesPascalCaseimplies that each word is written without a space and with a capital letter. Example:

TestName = 3

The PascalCase style is inherently very similar to thecamelCase, however, its history dates back to the Pascal language, all standard methods of which are named with a capital letter.

This style of writing names is widely used in many programming languages, for example, inJavaScriptAndPHPwhen writing class names.
