XP Methodology

MethodologyXP (Extreme Programming, Extreme Programming) implies an extreme strengthening of all development practices.

The main goal of the XP methodology is to improve development efficiency in an environment where product requirements are constantly changing. The use of XP gives good results, primarily when working with complex and uncertain projects.

The XP methodology is based on four main processes:

  • Coding.
  • Testing.
  • Design.
  • Hearing.
