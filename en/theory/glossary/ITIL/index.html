ITIL Guidelines

RecommendationsITIL (Information Technology Infrastructure Library, Information Technology Infrastructure Library) provide a package of recommendations for effectively combining IT services with business strategy.

Basic concepts of ITIL recommendations:

  • Customer focus in solving every problem.
  • Limiting excessive information for the user about all the difficulties and nuances of organizing technical support.
  • Preference for integrated services rather than hardware platforms;
  • Special tools should be widely implemented to organize the collaboration of all technical support employees.
  • Expanding the scope of interests of technical support, which, in addition to troubleshooting, should analyze the causes of their occurrence.
