Poker planning technique

TechniquePoker planning (Planning Poker) is intended to assess the volume and complexity of tasks that need to be implemented during software development. This assessment is carried out by a wide range of specialists in various fields - developers, testers, analysts, designers and other team members.

Work on the Poker planning technique includes the following stages:

  1. Selecting a task to evaluate.
  2. Distributing special cards to all team members or using online services (for example, Planningpoker.com) if the team is working remotely. Then you need to set values ​​for all cards and select a unit of measurement, for example, the time required to solve a problem can be estimated from0to5.
  3. The team is presented with a task and time is given for everyone to determine what value of the card in their opinion will correspond to the solution. Then everyone needs to place the selected card face down in sync with the rest of the participants.
  4. Everyone opens their cards and evaluates the result. Also, each team member explains why he chose this or that value. Next comes voting and a joint decision is made on the task at hand.
