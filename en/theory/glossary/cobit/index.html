Cobit methodology

MethodologyCobit (Control Objectives for Information and Related Technologies, Management tasks for information and related technologies) is a set of standards and guidelines in the field of IT auditing and management that are publicly available.

In IT management according to the Cobit methodology, the following stages can be distinguished:

  1. The management of business units builds an IT process strategy in accordance with the goals and tasks being implemented, as well as the creation of the very concept of the IT process.
  2. Formation by the management of IT departments of the company's policy, including ways and means of implementing previously set tasks.
  3. Develop standards for workflow steps.
  4. Formation of regulations for working on a project using previously developed standards.
