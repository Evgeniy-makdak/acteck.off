Tag relationships in HTML

In this lesson we will look at the terminology of tag relationships. Each tag, depending on its position in the HTML code, can be either a parent or a child, or both at the same time.

Consider the following code as an example:

<ul> <li>paragraph <i>italic</i></li> <li>paragraph <i>italic</i></li> <li>paragraph <i>italic</i></li> <li>paragraph <i>italic</i></li> </ul>

In this case we can say that tagsiin this code are descendants of tagsli, and are also descendants of the tagul. Moreover, for each of our tagsitagliis the immediate parent (father), and the tagul- grandfather.

What can you say about our tags?li? They are children of the taguland at the same time they themselves are parent tagsi. Well, what about the tag?ulis the parent of tagsliand tagsi. Moreover, for tagsliour tagul- immediate parent.
