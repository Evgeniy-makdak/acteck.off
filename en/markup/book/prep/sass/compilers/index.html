Compilers in SASS

Special programs are used to convert the codecompilers. There are quite a lot of such programs and you can choose one of them to suit your taste. To get started, I suggest you use one of the following programs:KoalaorPrepros.

You can also use an online compilerSASSmeister, where on the left we will write in the SCSS language, and on the right it will compile the code to CSS. However, it is worth remembering that it is, of course, not suitable for real work.

Install one of the suggested programs.
