dir attribute

Attributedirspecifies the text direction: left to right (default) or right to left.

Meaningltrspecifies the direction from left to right, and the valuertl- from right to left.

The text direction can also be changed using CSS propertiesunicode-bidiAnddirectionor tagsbdoAndbdi.

Example

Let's change the direction of the Russian text:

<p dir="rtl"> Этот текст имеет направление справа налево. </p>
