Reset form fields button

Taginputwith attributetypein meaningresetcreates a reset HTML fields buttonforms. After clicking on this button, the form fields will take on the appearance they had when entering the page (before the changes made by the user).

Example

Let's change the form fields and then click on the reset button to return them to their original state:

<form> <input type="checkbox" checked> <input type="text"> <input type="reset" value="кнопка сброса"> <input type="submit" value="кнопка отправки"> </form>
