optgroup tag

Tagoptgroupgroups itemsoptiondrop down listselect.

Grouping is needed to give a general title to a specific group of list items. This title is specified in the attributelabeltagoptgroup.

Attributes

Attribute Description
label Specifies the text that will be the title for a group of list items.

Example

Let's use the tagoptgroupLet's group the list items into two groups:

<select> <optgroup label="Country 1"> <option>City 1</option> <option>City 2</option> </optgroup> <optgroup label="Country 2"> <option>City 3</option> <option>City 4</option> </optgroup> </select>
