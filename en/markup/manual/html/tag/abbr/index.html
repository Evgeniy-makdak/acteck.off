Tag abbr

Tagabbrindicates that the text enclosed in the data tag is an abbreviation (abbreviationis an abbreviation of a name, usually typed in capital letters, for example, USA).

In attributetitlethis tag should be specifieddeciphering the abbreviation, and when you hover over the tag text, a tooltip will appear (as it should be for an attribute title).

Moreover, in this casetitleperforms another function -the decoding of the abbreviation will be indexed by search engines.

Some browsers may highlight the contents of this tag with a dotted underline.

Example

Let's put the abbreviation in the tagabbr, add an attributetitle, which provides a decoding of the abbreviation (hover your mouse over the abbreviation in the example and hold it for a few seconds - you will see a tooltip):

The country <abbr title="United States of America">USA</abbr> is located in North America.
