Conditional Groups in Vue

Sometimes we need to impose a condition on a group of tags at once. In this case, we can combine them with a common parent and impose a condition on it:

<template> <div v-if="isAuth"> <p>+++</p> <p>+++</p> <p>+++</p> </div> </template>

Sometimes we don't want to combine tags with a common parent. In this case, they can be combined with the tagtemplate. This tag combines other tags, but does not end up in the final layout. Let's put a condition on this tag:

<template> <template v-if="isAuth"> <p>+++</p> <p>+++</p> <p>+++</p> </template> </template>

Let's say you have three paragraphs and a button. Make these paragraphs disappear when you click a button.
