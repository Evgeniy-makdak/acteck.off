Webpack via ES modules in Webpack

By default, ES modules do not work in Webpack. They need to be turned on. To do this in the filepackage.jsonspecify setting"type"with meaning"module":

{ "type" : "module" }

In your working folder createpackage.jsonand enable ES modules in it.
