Level 10.1 of the JavaScript problem book

Make a function that takes a singular English noun as a parameter and returns it in the plural.

Make sure that when you click anywhere in the browser window, the sequence number of that click appears at the click location.

The text with punctuation marks is given:

'aaa bbb, ccc. Xxx - eee bbb, kkk!'

Get an array of words from such text.

Input given. As you enter numbers into it, it must separate triplets of numbers with spaces.

Two numbers are given. Display the process of multiplying these numbers in a column, just like in school.

Make a block that will move from the top of the screen when you press a button.
