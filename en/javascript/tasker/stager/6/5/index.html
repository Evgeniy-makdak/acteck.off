Level 6.5 of the JavaScript problem book

A paragraph and an input are given. The user's date of birth is entered into the input in the formatyear-month-day. Print in a paragraph how old the user is.

A paragraph and an input are given. Numbers separated by commas are entered into the input. Make sure that as you enter data into the input, the sum of the entered numbers is displayed in the paragraph.

Given a paragraph and a button. Each click on the button adds a new input. Numbers are entered into the inputs. Make sure that as you enter numbers into the inputs, the sum of the entered numbers is displayed in the paragraph.

An arbitrary number of inputs and a button are given. Numbers are entered into the inputs. By clicking on the button, find the sum of the entered numbers and display it in the text of the button.

Two inputs are given, a paragraph and a button. Numbers are entered into inputs separated by commas. By clicking on the button, output the numbers that are in both inputs, separated by commas, into a paragraph.
