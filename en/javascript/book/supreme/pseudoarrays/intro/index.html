Pseudo-arrays in JavaScript

In addition to arrays, JavaScript has objects that are similar to arrays, but do not support all of their capabilities. Such objects are calledpseudo-arrays.

Pseudo-arrays most often arise when working with DOM elements. Let's look at an example. Let us have paragraphs:

<p></p> <p></p> <p></p> <p></p> <p></p>

Let's get our paragraphs into a variable:

let elems = document.querySelectorAll('p');

In variableelemswill contain a pseudo-array:

console.log(elems); // это псевдомассив

In the following lessons, we will understand the features of pseudo-arrays, consider how they are similar to regular arrays, and how they differ.
