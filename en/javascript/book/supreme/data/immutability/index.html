Data Immutability in JavaScript

JavaScript often uses an approach where arrays and objects are not modified directly, but rather copied and then modified. In this case, arrays and objects are said to remain unchanged, that isimmutable.

This approach is often used in frameworks such as React. In this section of the tutorial, we will learn how to immutably modify arrays, objects, and arrays of objects. This knowledge will be useful to us in the future.
