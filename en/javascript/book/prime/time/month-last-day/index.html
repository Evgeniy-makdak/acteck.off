Finding the last day of the month in JavaScript

We use the automatic date adjustment we studied earlier to easily solve our problem. After all, in JavaScript, the last day of the month is the zero day of the next month.

For example, let’s determine the number of days in March2020year. To do this, when creating an object with a date, we indicate the zero day of April (it has the number3, since the numbering of months is from zero):

let date = new Date(2020, 3, 0); console.log(date.getDate());

Formulate this method of solving the problem as a function that will take the month and year as parameters and return the number of the last day of this month.
