Game of cities against a robot in JavaScript

Let's now modify our game so that the game is played against a robot, that is, a program, and not another person.

Let our robot have an array of all allowed cities:

let allCities = [];

When a person enters his city into the input, the robot will have to check the presence of this city in its array.

In this case, in each player’s turn the following problem situations are possible: the player entered a city that already existed; the player entered a city with a different letter than the previous city ends with; the player entered a city that is not on the robot's list.

On its turn, the robot must take one of the allowed cities so that this city is not already in the game. In this case, the selected city must begin with the letter with which the previous city ended.
