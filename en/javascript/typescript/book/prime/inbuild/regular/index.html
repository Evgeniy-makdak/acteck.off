Regulars in TypeScript

Let's make a variable containingregular expression:

let reg: RegExp;

Let's write a regular expression into it:

let reg: RegExp = /.+?/;

Or we’ll use an alternative way of specifying the regular sequence:

let reg: RegExp = new RegExp('.+?');
