Stickers on OOP with saving to local storage

In this lesson I will tell you the implementation of stickers in OOP. Stickers are notes with text that can be placed arbitrarily in a browser window. You can also change the size of stickers, move them around the screen with the mouse, and delete them. And, most importantly, any changes will be saved to the browser’s local storage and after refreshing the page, all your stickers will remain the same and in the same position!
