Math.asin method

MethodMath.asinreturns the arcsine of a number. The method parameter must be between-1to1, otherwise it will be returnedNaN.

Syntax

Math.asin(число);

Example

Let's print the arcsine of the number0.45.

console.log(Math.asin(0.45));

Result of executing the code:

0.4667653390472964

Example

Let's print the arcsine of the number-1:

console.log(Math.asin(-1));

Result of executing the code:

-1.5707963267948966

Example

Let's print the arcsine of the number45. Since the parameter is greater1, then it will be returnedNaN:

console.log(Math.asin(45));

Result of executing the code:

NaN
