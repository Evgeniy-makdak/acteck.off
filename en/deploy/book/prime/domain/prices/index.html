Domain prices

As a rule, prices for these domains are also visible on domain occupancy check services. Let's discuss the nuances of pricing.

Prices in one zone

You may notice that domain prices vary depending on the zone. However, even within the same zone, domain prices may vary. Let's see what's going on here.

Option 1. The fact is that you are offered to buypremiumdomain - with a short or beautiful name.

Option 2. You may also be offered to buy pre-owned domains. Such domains are more expensive because they already have links from other sites, some traffic, and these domains have some weight from search engines. Scheme for professionals! The fact is that such a domain could, for example, get banned by search engines. Purchasing such a domain will result in you not being able to promote it.

Discounts

There are still nuances. You may notice that some domain zones offer discounts. This is a small deception scheme on the part of the registrars. The fact is that the discount is valid only for the first year. And then you will have to either give up the domain or pay its full price.
