Domain levels

Domains are classified as differentlevelsdepending on the number of dots inside the full domain name.

If there is one point in the domain, for example,site.com, then this is the domainsecondlevel. If there are two points in the domain, for example,blog.site.com, then this is the domainthirdlevel or, in other words,subdomain.

The question arises, where is the first level domain? This includes the domain zone itself, in our case.com.

Domains can be of a higher level: fourth, fifth, but this happens extremely rarely.

What level is the next domain?example.com?

What level is the next domain?blog.example.com?

What level is the next domain?my.blog.example.com?
