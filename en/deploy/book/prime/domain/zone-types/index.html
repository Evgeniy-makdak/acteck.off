Types of domain zones

Domain zones can be divided into several types. Let's figure out what these types are.

National zones

Each country has its own domain zone. For example,.rufor Russia,.ukfor UK,.usfor USA.

Countries that have already ceased to exist also have domain zones. For example, zone.sufor the Soviet Union.

Regional zones

There are domain zones for some regions. For example,.eufor the European Union,.asiafor Asia,.moscowfor Moscow.

International zones

Initially, general, international zones were introduced for sites on a certain topic. For example,.comfor commercial organizations,.infofor information sites,.netfor network services.

Currently, division into categories is not observed and any sites are located in these zones.

Thematic zones

Not long ago, a whole host of domains appeared for various topics. For example,.art, .shop, .club.
