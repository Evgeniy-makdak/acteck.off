Registering a domain for a website

To get a domain, you need to buy it fromregistrar. A registrar is a special organization that allows you to register your domain by selecting it from among the unoccupied domains.

Technically, a registrar is a website where you can select a free domain and register it in your name or a legal entity.

As a rule, second-level domains are purchased. Having a second-level domain, you can create any number of third-level domains based on it for free.

It happens that third-level domains are also sold, for example, in the zoneof.byorcom.ua.

Domain prices are in order10$per year, but may be slightly less and significantly more.
