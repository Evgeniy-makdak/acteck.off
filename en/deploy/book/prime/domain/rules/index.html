Characters allowed in domains

Initially, Latin characters, numbers and hyphens were allowed for domain names.

Then, after some time, symbols from national alphabets were allowed. For example, currently you can register a domain in Cyrillic, for example, like this:магазин.ru.

Let's see what restrictions there are on domain names:

  • The name length must be from2to63characters.
  • Character case does not matter. For example, domainsmysite.comAndMySite.comare considered the same.
  • The name must not contain hyphens at the same time.3th and4th positions.
  • The name must not begin or end with a hyphen.
