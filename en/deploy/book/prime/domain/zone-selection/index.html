Selecting a zone for a domain

Let's now discuss how to choose the right zone for your new domain. You need to consider the following things: the topic of the site, the expected geography of your potential visitors, the availability of free domains in the zone, the price of the domain in the selected zone.

If you're targeting an audience within one country, then that country's national zone is a good choice.

If the geography of your site fits one of the regional zones, then you can choose that too. For example, if you have a website only for Moscow residents, then the zone.moscowwould also be a good choice.

If you assume that the geography of your site will grow in the future, then it is better to choose an international or thematic zone.

You can choose a thematic area, even if you are focusing on only one country.

Some zones, for example the zone.itare national (Italy), but due to the beautiful combination of letters they are used throughout the world. In this case, the zone.itwell suited for an IT website.

There is also a third nuance. In older areas, e.g..comalmost all the good names are already taken. Therefore, it makes sense to consider newer zones.

There is also a fourth nuance. The fact is that domains in different zones have different prices, often an order of magnitude higher than in other zones. It’s important to strike a balance and think about whether you’re willing to pay more
