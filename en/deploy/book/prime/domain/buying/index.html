Buying a domain

Let's now move on to purchasing your first domain. As you already know, this is done on the registrar’s website.

There are usually many registrars in each country, but there is usually the most important, oldest and most trusted one. It is best to buy a domain from such a registrar, and it would be better if the registrar is located in your country.

For example, for Belarus such a registrar ishoster.by, and for Russia -reg.ru. If you live in another country, then Google which registrar in your country is the most important.

So, now you are faced with the task of buying your first domain. We will use it later when going through the tutorial. But since domains are purchased for at least a year, it would be foolish to buy a domain just for school. Therefore, it is better to foresee in advance how you will use this domain in the future. I would advise using it for a portfolio site and calling it accordingly - with your name.

For Belarus I have a promotional code for a small discount fromhoster.by. A promotional code was issued specifically for readers of this textbook. Here it is: U23YQ5S8. To use it, enter it into the appropriate field when registering a domain.

Choose a name for your first site by checking if it is empty.

Go to the registrar website in your country and purchase the domain of your choice.
