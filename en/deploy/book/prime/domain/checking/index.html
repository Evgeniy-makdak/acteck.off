Domain occupancy check

Let's now learn how to check domains for availability. For this, each registrar has its own service. For example:hoster.by, reg.ru, domain.com.

Such services provide an input field into which you enter the desired domain name. After a short time, the service provides information about whether the domain is busy or not.

If a domain is busy, such services often offer similar unoccupied names. Sometimes they are very successful, sometimes not. In any case, pay attention to them.

If you play around with checking domains a little, you will see that most of the beautiful domains are already taken, especially in popular old zones.comor.net.

In this regard, choosing a domain name is now an art. It is necessary that the domain is beautiful and that the name is not taken.

If you need a certain domain, and its name is already taken in some zone, you can look for this name in another zone, less popular or more expensive.

Come up with several domain names. Check to see if they are busy.
