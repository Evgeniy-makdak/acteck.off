Enable HTTPS for the site

Currently, a site must have an SSL certificate in order for the site to operate over HTTPS.

In general, SSL certificates are paid and someone makes good money from this, because you can use free certificates that work just as well as paid ones.

The free certificates are called Let's Encrypt. To make your site work via HTTPS, you just need to go to certificate management in your hosting control panel and click a button there that will have text like “enable Let's Encrypt”

If your hosting does not support free Let's Encrypt certificates and offers you paid ones, this is a bad hosting. Move from it to normal.

A site-specific Let's Encrypt certificate is issued to3month. Then it needs to be extended. But hosting does this automatically, we don’t need to worry about it.

Enable a free SSL certificate for your site. Check that the site is accessible viahttps://.
