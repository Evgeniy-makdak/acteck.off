Hosting technical support

All paid hosting services have technical support. If you can’t do something and googling doesn’t help, immediately write to support for help. As a rule, support responds quickly enough and helps resolve the problem.

Sometimes it happens that the problem is generally on the hosting side and without contacting support you would not be able to solve it.

Once again: do not hesitate to write to support! After all, you have a paid account and support’s job is to help you!

Find the section in your hosting control panel where you can write to support.
