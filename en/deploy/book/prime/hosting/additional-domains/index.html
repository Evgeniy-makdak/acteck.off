Additional domains on VH

When you buy shared hosting, you should already have one domain, which is immediately linked to the hosting and becomesmain.

However, if your plan includes multiple domains, you can park your other domains as well. Such domains are calledadditional.

Let's figure out how to park your additional domain on your hosting. We will consider the simplest case, when the registrar coincides with the hosting.

In this case, you need to register a domain, wait until it is delegated, and then in the hosting control panel in the domain management section, simply add a new domain.

Buy a second domain. Wait for him to delegate.

Add your domain in your hosting control panel.

Upload a test site to your new domain via FTP.

Enable a free SSL certificate for your new domain. Check that the site is accessible viahttps://.
