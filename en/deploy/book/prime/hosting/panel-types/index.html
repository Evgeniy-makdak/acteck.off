Types of hosting control panels

As you already know, after payment you will have access tohosting control panels. This panel is a separate program that was developed by another company - not the hoster. The hoster simply bought a license for it.

There are three popular panels: CPanel, ISPManager and Plesk. Your hosting will most likely have one of them installed.

These panels are paid. Moreover, payment is made by subscription - monthly. But in the case of shared hosting, the hoster pays for the panel, and the cost is already included in your tariff.

But this is not necessary. There are hostings that, for example, have a CPanel panel installed, and for an additional (monthly!) fee you can install, for example, ISPManager. Typically you don't need this option. This is such a small scam on the part of the hoster. Be careful!
