What do we need from hosting?

Let's write a list of what we need from hosting:

  • linking your domain
  • hard drive space for website files
  • hosting control panel
  • SSL certificate
  • database
  • mail for domain
  • creating subdomains

Later in the textbook we will discuss the nuances of obtaining services from the above list.
