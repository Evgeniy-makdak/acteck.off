Shared hosting tariffs

Virtual hosting can be paid or free. In case of paid hosting we pay for:

  • number of sites
  • number of databases
  • hard drive space

There are no fees for hosting resources. There are resource restrictions for the site, but within these restrictions the site can consume varying amounts of resources and the fee for this, as a rule, does not increase.

Typically, all hosting services have a range of tariffs from simpler to more advanced.
