Subdomains on VH

Let's now learn how to create subdomains. To do this, in your hosting control panel, go to the domain management section. Create a new domain there, but giving it a name as a subdomain from your current domain.

When creating a subdomain, you will be asked to specify the folder in the hosting file system in which your domain will be located. You can leave the default value or set your own.

After creating a subdomain, it will start working almost immediately (no more than ten minutes). You will be able to connect via FTP to your hosting file system and upload the files of your new site.

Create a subdomain of your main site. Check its availability through your browser.

Upload the test site files to your subdomain. Check that everything is working.

Enable a free SSL certificate for your subdomain. Check that the site is accessible viahttps://.
