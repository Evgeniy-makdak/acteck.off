Free Git Pages Hosting

There are special free hosting services that are more trusted. One of them is hostingGit Pages. It allows you to launch websites directly from your GitHub repository. To do this, a subdomain is automatically created that works throughhttps://.

The advantages of such hosting include the convenience of display and the fact that it is not blocked by messengers and social networks. The disadvantages of such hosting include the fact that your domain will not be of the second level. In addition, this hosting only supports static.

In general, this hosting is a good option for static websites for a layout designer’s portfolio or front-end.
