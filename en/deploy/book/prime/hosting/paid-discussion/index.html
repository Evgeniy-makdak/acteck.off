Discussion of virtual hostings

The advantage of shared hosting is its simplicity. You practically don't need to configure anything yourself. The hoster will simply give you access to your folder on the hosting and you will upload site files there. These files will work immediately.

The downside of shared hosting is the resource limitation. A website is allocated a limited amount of hard disk space, a certain amount of RAM, and a certain amount of CPU time. There may also be restrictions on Internet traffic to the site.

You should also keep in mind that shared hosting is mainly hosting for PHP sites with SQL databases. Rare hostings have support for NodeJS and Python and it is limited. Websites in such languages ​​are usually hosted on servers.

In general, such hosting is a good option for any low-load project with bare static data or PHP. Such hosting can host a landing page, blog, forum, online store and other types of sites.
