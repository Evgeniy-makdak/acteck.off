Discussion of free hosting

In addition to paid virtual hosting, there are alsofree. Let's discuss their advantages and disadvantages.

They have only one advantage - they are free. But there are many shortcomings and at present they are quite critical:

Third level domains

On free hosting, you are given a domain for free, but these will be third-level domains.

Of course, the fact that the domain is free is a plus. But this domain will not belong to you - this is a minus. If something happens to the hosting - for example, it closes or changes its policy, then your domain will be lost. But it could be promoted, visitors could come to it, and so on.

It should be noted that there are free hosting services that allow you to park your second-level domains purchased from a third-party registrar.

Problems with certificates

On free hosting, most likely the domain will not have an SSL certificate.

And browsers don’t really trust such domains, displaying all sorts of unpleasant exclamation marks in the address bar, scaring site visitors.

In addition, modern messengers and social networks block transitions to sites operating throughhttp://. Which is a huge problem. After all, you can’t even post work for a portfolio on such a site, since a potential employer, when switching from the messenger, will not be able to access it.

Advertising

Advertising and pop-ups. Bad free hosting providers often place advertisements on their clients' sites, which can annoy visitors and reduce conversions.

Lack of support

Free hosting providers usually do not provide technical support to their customers. In general, this is not very important, but with support it is still more convenient.

Problem with mail

Free hosting usually does not have email support for the site. That is, your site will not even be able to send the user, for example, a letter about successful registration.

Professional image

The use of paid hosting indicates the seriousness and reliability of your project.

PHP only

As a rule, free hosting services only support static and PHP sites.
