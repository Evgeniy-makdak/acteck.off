Introduction to Hosting

In this section we will begin to study the layout of the site onhosting.

Hosting is a special place to host a website so that it is accessible via the Internet. Hosting services are provided by companieshosters.

In practice, hosting is a website where you register and get the opportunity to host your websites.

Often the registrar and hoster are one organization. That is, where you registered the site, you can buy hosting. But this is not mandatory. You can register a site in one place and host it in another, and if you wish, move to another hosting.
