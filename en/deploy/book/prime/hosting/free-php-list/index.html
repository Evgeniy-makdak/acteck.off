List of free hosting for PHP

In this lesson I will provide a list of free hosting services that I have Googled at the moment. It is worth noting here that hosting, which is free now, will most likely be paid in the future. Free is a way to promote new hosting.

And, as I wrote earlier, free hosting only supports static and PHP sites.

So here's the list:sweb.ru, freehosting.com, sprinthost.ru, infinityfree.com, hostiman.ru.

Study the conditions on the presented free hosting services.

Register on one of the free hosting services. Upload your test site to it.
