Prices for paid hosting

Average price for paid hosting - approx.10$per month. For this amount you can place5-10second level domains and an infinite number of their subdomains.

The exact amount you'll pay depends on the hosting you choose.

Also, each hosting usually has a range of tariffs, ranging from the most basic to the steepest. Accordingly, the price for hosting increases.

Also, almost all hosting services offer discounts if you buy hosting for a longer period. As a rule, the discounts are quite interesting and it is more profitable to buy hosting for a year or more than to pay for it every month.

You can also save money if you find it somewherepromotional code. It is a special code that is distributed along with hosting advertising. When paying for hosting, you can enter this code and get a discount from5to15percent.
