Types of hosting by type of service

Hosting services can be divided into two types:virtualhosting andserver.

Virtualhosting is regular hosting, when your site is hosted on the hoster’s computer next to other similar sites.Server- this is hosting, within which you get a whole computer on which one or more of your sites will be hosted.

As a rule, both of these options are provided by the same hoster. Each of these options has its pros and cons. We will continue to study virtual hosting later in this tutorial, and server hosting will be described in a separate tutorial.
