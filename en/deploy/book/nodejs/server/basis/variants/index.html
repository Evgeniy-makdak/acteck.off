Hosting options for NodeJS projects

Laying out projects in NodeJS has its own characteristics and is much more complicated than laying out a regular HTML site or a site in PHP.

Various hosting options are available: free cloud hosting onheroku.com, paid virtual hosting onhoster.by, paid virtual servers onruvds.com.

Since the first two options have their limitations, we will explore virtual server hosting (VPS).
