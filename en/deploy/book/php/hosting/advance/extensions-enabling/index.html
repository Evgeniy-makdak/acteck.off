Enabling PHP modules on hosting

Let's now figure out what to do if the module you need is not available on your hosting. In this case, you can try to enable it in your hosting control panel.

Not all hosting providers allow you to enable additional modules. And whoever allows it will not have all the modules. That is, your module may not be on the list of available ones for inclusion.

Let's try it. Look for module settings in your hosting control panel. Typically they are called PHP Extensions. These settings can be hidden quite deeply. For example, in the PHP language version settings.
