The difference between PHP on local and hosting

It often happens that a script that works great on your computer stops working when uploaded to hosting.

The reasons may be the following: differences in PHP versions, lack of PHP modules, differences in settings in php.ini, incorrect paths to files and folders, differences in Windows and Linux file systems.

In the following lessons we will take a closer look at the problems and how to fix them.
